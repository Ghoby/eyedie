Eye Die is an adventure side-scroller game, set in a futuristic and dystopian city of Los Angeles, in the vein of games like Grim Fandango and Night In The Woods. It also follows the vein of some movies like Blade Runner and Chinatown.

The game has the goal of using its storytelling focused mechanics and structure to make a world in which the player can immerse himself and live out a story filled with intrigue, mystery and melancholy.
